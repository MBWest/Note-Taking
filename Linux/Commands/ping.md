# Ping

> ## **Syntax**

- ping [IP] or URL

---

> ## **Example**

| **Command**   | **Description**   | 
| --------------|-------------------|
| `ping [IP] or URL` | Send ICMP packets to the IP address |

---
---

> ## **Options**

| **Option** |**Description**   | 
| --------------|-------------------|
| `-c` | Assign a count of how many times you want the ping command sent out|
